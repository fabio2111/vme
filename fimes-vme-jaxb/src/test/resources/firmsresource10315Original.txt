Projections were limited to five years as extended projections are increasingly driven by recruitment assumptions. Spawner biomass was projected assuming <i>F</i> = 0, and under recently observed fishing mortality (<i>F</i> = 0.14). If there are no removals, spawner biomass is projected to increase 57% by 2012.
